# Fixture Attributes Reference

Common controllable attributes found in lighting fixtures.

---

## Intensity & Output

**Dimmer**  
Controls overall brightness (usually HTP).

**Shutter**  
Opens/closes light output mechanically or digitally.

**Strobe**  
Rapid on/off flashing; often part of shutter channel.

---

## Position (Moving Fixtures)

**Pan**  
Horizontal movement.

**Tilt**  
Vertical movement.

**Pan/Tilt Fine**  
High-resolution control (16-bit).

---

## Color

**RGB / RGBW / RGBA / RGBL**  
Color mixing systems using LEDs.

**Color Wheel**  
Discrete color filters.

---

## Beam & Optics

**Zoom**  
Beam angle control.

**Focus**  
Sharpness of projected image.

**Frost**  
Softens beam edges.

**Iris**  
Reduces beam diameter mechanically.

---

## Gobo & Effects

**Gobo**  
Pattern inserted into beam.

**Gobo Rotation**  
Spin or index gobo.

**Prism**  
Splits beam into multiple images.

**Prism Rotation**  
Rotates prism effect.

---

## Movement Enhancements

**Speed**  
Rate of movement or transitions.

**Reset**  
Fixture reboot or calibration command. (Never used while performing a show)
